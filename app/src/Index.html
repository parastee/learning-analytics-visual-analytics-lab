How to use our platform

This Guid is going to teach you apply with Us ( AG ).

Read details below to learn more Apply Germany.

Fill out the information form and click on "Find Uni" to find your desired university using a map.

By clicking on the name of the city and then on the button "SELECT" or simply search the name of the city on the text box you can observe the universities available in this city. When you click on the university, the pop-up list will appear. For more information about the selected university, click on "Read more". If the university shown suits you, click on "Add to comparison list", otherwise click on "No, thanks". In this phase you can only select five universities. Then click on the "COMPARE" button to display further details of selected universities in various diagrams.

Charts:

Radar Chart is identify the most and lowest area of study in the choosen universities.

On the Stacked Bar Chart , you will find the number of Bachelor and Master students in relation to the universities that you have already selected.

The Area Spline Chart compares universites in order to support students.

At the end, simply select the right universities using the check box and click on the "Confirm and Apply" button. Confirm your information and go to the Applying phase.
